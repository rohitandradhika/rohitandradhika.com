Wedding Party

Our wedding party consists of our lovely bridesmaids and groomsmen. The bridesmaids are Radhika's cousins and best friend, and the groomsmen are Rohit's cousins and a secret groomsman. These individuals are very close to us and we would love to include them even further in our wedding.

Although Radhika's brothers and Rohit's sisters aren't included in the wedding party formally, in our hearts they are as much a part of this wedding as the individuals listed below!

Manisha Fernando

Maid of Honour

Manisha has been Radhika's best friend for the past 10 years. Their relationship is one where even separated and unable to meet all the time, they are the closest friends you will ever see! They have been there for each other through thick and thin. They can say anything to each other and not have to worry about it hurting the other. Radhika truly loves Manisha like a sister, and can't even think about having her wedding without Manisha as her maid of honour.

She is one of the sweetest, most caring individuals Radhika knows and has a tendency to nurture everyone around her. She also gives great advice! She is beautiful inside and out. Although they barely get to see each other since they live so far apart, she is considered very close to Radhika in her heart. She plays a very essential role in this wedding!
