Attractions

Listed here are all the exciting places to visit for all of the out of town guests:

Canada's Wonderland

905-832-8131

9580 Jane Street

Vaughan, Ontario L6A1S6

View Website

View Map

Experience the excitement at Canada's Wonderland with many many rides, places to eat, and lots of fun!! Definitely not a place you want to miss if you are a fan of roller coasters and heart dropping rides!

Niagara Falls

1-888-325-5788

6380 Fallsview Boulevard

Niagara Falls, Ontario L2G7X5

View Map

See the famous Niagara Falls and enjoy your time at the Casino nearby. Haunted houses, arcade games, places to eat, and much more are also there for families to enjoy nearby.

Toronto Islands

416-397-2628

9 Queens Quay West

Toronto, Ontario M5J2V3

View Website

View Map

A place for all the nature lovers! Take the ferry to the Toronto Islands and enjoy the greenery, the lake, and the activities. Centre Island specifically is our favourite to make a visit to on a yearly basis.

Toronto Eaton Centre

416-598-8560

220 Yonge Street

Toronto, Ontario M5B2H1

View Website

View Map

The largest mall in downtown Toronto, holding every kind of store to cater to all of your needs. Definitely a place for the shopaholics to visit.

Toronto Zoo

416-392-5929

361 Old Finch Avenue

Toronto, Ontario M1B5K7

View Website

View Map

For all animal lovers, this zoo has every animal you could possibly want to see and the indoor pavilions allow you to visit during any weather. A place where you can see the beautiful creatures from all over the world.

Swaminarayan Temple

416-798-2277

61 Claireville Drive

Toronto, Ontario M9W5Z7

View Website

View Map

Visit this beautiful hand carved mandir, where all the marble has come from India, and each and every nook of the temple has attention to detail. So serene and magnificent, not one to miss!

CN Tower

416-868-6937

301 Front Street West

Toronto, Ontario M5V2T6

View Website

View Map

The main attraction of Toronto with it's revolving restaurant, glass floor, 'edgewalk', and much more. It is definitely a place for all newcomers to Toronto to visit...unless of course you're afraid of heights!
