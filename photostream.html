Wedding App

We have an app for our wedding!

To install the app:
1. Download "Wedding Party" from the App Store
2. Type "rohitandradhika" into the app when asked
3. Take pictures and share on the app!

Now we get to see our wedding from your eyes! Please share your pictures, stories, and comments...and have fun while doing it! It's also a great way to have our wedding details in your hands on the go!

Android Users: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.micromobs.pkk
iPhone Users: https://itunes.apple.com/ca/app/wedding-party-getting-married/id494214305?mt=8

If you don't have an Android or iPhone...get one!! (Kidding). You can see the uploaded pictures at this website: https://www.weddingpartyapp.com/e/iXCLe33

Launch photo stream in a new window.
